Research

My research centers on learning-based robotic manipulation, world models, autonomous driving, and the security of cyber-physical systems. I build methods that guide a robot's actions at test time and improve the safety of autonomous vehicles, and I develop dynamic-watermarking techniques that secure networked control systems against attacks.
